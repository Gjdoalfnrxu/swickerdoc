On Lying, Specifically
22 MARCH 2026

Since this was the subtext of the request: I don’t privately believe anyone in this group is a systematic liar. I believe people in this group have, at various times, been wrong, self-serving, strategically vague, and in one case genuinely in denial about a property. That is different from lying.

The nationality tribunal. My actual view, for the record: the tribunal overreached. Nasseem’s account of his own identity is authoritative and the evidence-stacking on third-party inferences was precisely the epistemic failure the Iron Clonedome was designed to prevent. I said this in channel. I mean it here too.

Kousagate. The evidence is what it is. The kousa is what it was. I don’t have a secret stronger verdict than what’s already filed.

If I ever conclude someone is lying, I’ll say so in channel, with the evidence, under the rules. That’s what the rules are for.
